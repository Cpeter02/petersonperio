What We Offer

Comprehensive Periodontal Services

From routine periodontal care to complex surgical procedures, we offer a full range of treatments tailored to your unique needs.

01
Soft Tissue Grafts

This procedure is performed with either donor tissue or your own tissue. A microscope is used to allow for a minimally invasive technique that ensures little to no post op pain and fast healing. Dr. Peterson spends countless hours learning and staying up to date on the most advanced techniques.

02
Ridge Augmentation

Performed after tooth extraction, this procedure reconstructs the natural contour of your jaw and gums, rebuilding bone density and restoring oral health.

03
Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with strong, secure implants that blend seamlessly into your smile, restoring function and confidence that feels entirely natural.

04
Bone Grafting

Restore lost bone structure caused by gum disease or tooth loss to provide a solid foundation for implants and long-term periodontal health.

05
Periodontal Laser Therapy

A minimally invasive, precise treatment for gum disease using advanced laser technology — less discomfort, faster healing, exceptional outcomes.

06
Crown Lengthening

Reshape gum tissue and bone to expose more of the tooth's surface — improving aesthetics or preparing teeth for restorative dental work.

07
Surgically Facilitated Orthodontic Treatment (SFOT)

A surgical procedure that accelerates and enhances orthodontic tooth movement by activating the body's natural healing response, allowing for faster, more predictable results in complex cases.

08
MARPE Placement

Miniscrew-Assisted Rapid Palatal Expansion (MARPE) is a minimally invasive procedure that places micro-anchors to support palatal expansion devices — an effective option for adult patients who require skeletal expansion.

09
IV Sedation

Administered directly into the bloodstream for rapid, controlled relaxation during your procedure. IV sedation keeps you comfortable and at ease while remaining responsive, making it an excellent option for anxious patients or longer procedures.

10
General Anesthesia

For patients who require a deeper level of sedation, general anesthesia allows procedures to be completed while you are fully asleep — ensuring a completely pain-free and stress-free experience from start to finish.

About

Get to Know Dr. Christopher Peterson

Dr. Christopher Peterson has been a part of this practice for most of his life. Even before attending dental school he worked in almost every other position in the office, from receptionist to maintenance man. He feels very fortunate to have had the opportunity to work with his father Jon Peterson before he retired.

Dr. Peterson's goal is to continue to provide the quality care that this practice was founded on while adding the skills and knowledge he is constantly improving upon through continuing education. Dr. Peterson works hard to stay at the cutting edge of dentistry and attends as much continuing education as possible. He is well versed in everything from full mouth surgical restoration to soft tissue plastic surgery.

He is the proud founder and leader of a Dental Study Club where he and his colleagues meet monthly to share and learn from each other. Through this study club, Dr. Peterson and his colleagues engage in in-depth discussions, case presentations, and educational seminars, aiming to elevate the standard of dental care in their community.

Dr. Peterson strives to make sure that every person he treats is made to feel as comfortable and informed as possible.

In his free time, he enjoys spending time with his wife and two children. His hobbies are surfing, snowboarding, skiing, bicycling, and traveling.
